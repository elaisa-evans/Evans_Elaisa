Baluarte de San Diego

In Intramuros, the Baluarte de San Diego is a magnificent park with exquisite gardens and old fort ruins. It was known by its famous bastion where it used to be a drowning chamber during the Spanish era and designed to repel invasion. The Baluarte de San Diego is a popular venue for weddings, parties, and other special occasions since it is scenic beauty gardens with lush foliage, paths, fountains, and a picturesque pergola that will remind you of a magical realm, where most people may roam around it.
