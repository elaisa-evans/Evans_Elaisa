Museo de Intramuros

If you want to learn more about Intramuros' history, go to the Museo de Intramuros, which was constructed to provide more information about the Walled City's past. The main museum is a three-story former mission house where it has six components to exhibit: The Immaculate Conception, The Religious Order, The Patronato Real, and the Establishment of Parishes are on the first floor; The Establishment of a Parish and Sacred Vessels, The Indio Response, and Religious Colonial Paintings are on the second floor; and an exhibition on the history and rebirth of Intramuros is on the third floor. It basically houses 500 religious relics discovered in historic churches around Intramuros.
