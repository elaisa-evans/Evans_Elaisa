Bahay Tsinoy

Do you want to know how China influences the Philippines? You might want to visit Bahay Tsinoy, a museum dedicated to the history and lifestyles of Chinese people in the Philippines. The museum is well-designed, with several life-like displays depicting Chinese Filipinos' daily lives in the past. It also includes detailed information on the culinary, ecclesiastical, and linguistic history. Its purpose is to promote understanding between the Chinese and Filipino societies while also offering a new platform for patriotic campaigns in the Philippines.
